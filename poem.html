A poem about the Arya's

By Lachlan Lalor

Two young boys, both named Arya,
Chased the wind through fields of marigold.
Laughter echoed, wild and free,
Two bright flames, brave and bold.

Hand in hand, they raced the sun,
Through rivers, dreams, and endless skies.
No names could ever set apart
The kindred spark behind their eyes.
