Buy Your Tickets

Select your ticket type, enter your details, apply a promo code (if you have one), and proceed to checkout. Each ticket comes with unique benefits for your festival experience!

Ticket Types

Standard Ticket

Price: $20

Enjoy access to the main stage, food vendors, and general festival areas. This is perfect for music lovers who want to experience the festival without the premium perks.

VIP Ticket

Price: $100

Get exclusive VIP access to premium areas including a VIP lounge, front-row seating, and fast-track entry to shows. Plus, enjoy free drinks and snacks throughout the festival!

Student Ticket

Price: $10

Special discount for students! Get access to the main stage and all festival areas at a reduced rate. Please present valid student ID upon entry.

Family Bundle

Price: $75 (for 5 people)

A great deal for families! This bundle includes 5 standard tickets for the price of 4. Enjoy the festival with your loved ones and make lasting memories!

Ultra VIP (VVVIP)

Price: $500

The ultimate festival experience! Includes all VIP perks, plus backstage passes, meet-and-greets with artists, and a luxury VIP tent for your comfort. Only a limited number of VVVIP tickets available.

Total Price: $20
