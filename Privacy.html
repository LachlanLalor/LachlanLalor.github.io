At Coastline Capriccio, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we safeguard it. By using our website, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy. Please read it carefully to understand how we handle your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information to provide you with a better experience on our website, including:

1.1 Personal Information

When you interact with our site, we may ask you to provide certain personally identifiable information, such as:

  • Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Billing Information (if applicable)
  • Any other information you provide when contacting us

1.2 Usage Data

We may also collect data on how you access and use our website. This data includes:

  • Your IP Address
  • Browser Type and Version
  • Device Information (e.g., mobile or desktop)
  • Pages Visited and Time Spent on the Site
  • Operating System Details
  • Referring URL (the page from which you arrived at our website)

1.3 Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your experience on our site. Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device. These help us understand how you use our website, remember your preferences, and improve site functionality. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may limit your ability to use some features of our site.

1.4 Third-Party Integrations

Our website may include third-party services, such as analytics providers or social media integration. These third-party services may collect information about you. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these services for more information on how they collect and process your data.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used in the following ways:

2.1 To Improve User Experience

We use your personal and usage data to personalize your experience on our website, making it easier for you to navigate and interact with our services.

2.2 To Communicate with You

We may use your contact information to respond to inquiries, provide support, or inform you about important updates regarding our services, policies, or products.

2.3 To Process Transactions

If you make a purchase or donation, we may use your personal information to process your payment and fulfill your order. Payment details, including billing addresses, are processed securely through trusted payment processors.

2.4 To Improve Website Functionality

We use usage data to analyze how visitors interact with our site. This helps us identify areas for improvement, optimize content, and enhance the overall user experience.

2.5 For Marketing and Promotional Purposes

If you have opted-in to receive marketing materials from us, we may use your contact information to send you promotional offers, updates, or newsletters. You can opt-out at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link provided in our emails or by contacting us directly.

3. How We Protect Your Information

We take the security of your personal data seriously and implement a range of security measures to protect it. However, please be aware that no method of data transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

3.1 Data Encryption

We use encryption technology (SSL/TLS) to secure sensitive data, such as payment information, during transmission.

3.2 Secure Storage

Your personal information is stored in secure servers with restricted access. We take all reasonable precautions to protect your data from unauthorized access or disclosure.

3.3 Third-Party Service Providers

We may work with third-party service providers to help us process data, such as payment processors, email marketing services, and cloud storage. These service providers are contractually obligated to handle your information securely and in compliance with applicable data protection laws.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your data in the following situations:

4.1 With Service Providers

We may share your information with trusted third-party vendors who assist us in operating our website or performing services on our behalf, such as payment processing, email communication, and data analytics.

4.2 Legal Compliance

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or if we believe that such action is necessary to comply with legal obligations, protect our rights, or prevent fraud.

4.3 Business Transfers

If we undergo a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your personal information may be transferred to the new owner as part of that transaction.
